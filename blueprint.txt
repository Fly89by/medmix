# PROJECT NAME

MED.MIX OS

# COMPANY

Al Madinah Ready Mix (MED.MIX)

# GOAL

Build a complete AI-powered CRM and Sales Management Platform for MED.MIX.

The platform must:

* Manage leads
* Manage companies
* Manage contacts
* Manage projects
* Generate quotations
* Automate follow-ups
* Provide analytics
* Provide AI assistance
* Store company knowledge

The goal is to reduce manual sales work by at least 80%.
